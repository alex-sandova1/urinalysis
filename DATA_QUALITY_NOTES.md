Dataset is from UTI diagnosis.

Blank entry found for urine color, it will not be removed. that entry will be flagged and wont be used for color analysis.

Age is grouped as follows: 0-17 as Under 18, 18 to 25 as Young Adult, 26 to 64 as Adult, and 65+ as Seniors.

Reference values for paremeters are as follows: (they will be used to flag abnormal values in the dataset and updated as needed)

Color
    Normal: Pale Yellow – Amber

    Abnormal: Red, Brown, Green, Blue, Orange, Milky, etc.

Transparency (Appearance)
    Normal: Clear

    Abnormal: Cloudy/Turbid

Glucose
    Normal: Negative

    Abnormal: Trace, 1+, 2+, 3+, 4+

Protein

    Normal: Negative

    Abnormal: Trace or Positive

pH

    Normal: 4.5 – 8.0

    Abnormal: <4.5 or >8.0

Specific Gravity

    Normal: 1.005 – 1.030

    Abnormal: <1.005 or >1.030

WBC

    Normal: 0–5 cells/HPF

    Abnormal: >5

RBC

    Normal: 0–2 cells/HPF

    Abnormal: >2

Epithelial Cells

    Normal: 0–5 cells/HPF (few)

    Abnormal: Moderate or Many

Mucus Threads

    Normal: None – Few

    Abnormal: Moderate or Many

Amorphous Urates

    Normal: None – Few

    Abnormal: Moderate or Many

Bacteria

    Normal: None

    Abnormal: Few, Moderate, Many
